Toyota · 2024

Enhancing dealership operations with effortless fleet management

Toyota is a client that I worked with at my previous co-op (Invoke Co.) and we have collaborated on a wide range of different projects. In this project, my team and I worked closely with Dolan Toyota to create visual artifacts for their fleet management system. The focus was to update their current ordering system, adhering to the admins request of creating an efficient and aesthetic experience.

Platforms Figma
Tasks User Research, Prototyping, Usability Testing
Deliverables User Flows, Mockups, Final Prototype
Team Product Designer

problem

The existing fleet management system has outdated UI and new admins find it difficult to learn. Setting order limits on vehicles and viewing existing limits are unintuitive in the existing UI. How might we design a more intuitive fleet management system that empowers new administrators to efficiently manage vehicle order limits?

process

User Research

I conducted user interviews with dealership staff to understand their day-to-day workflows, pain points, and mental models around vehicle tracking. Key findings revealed that staff relied heavily on spreadsheets and verbal communication, leading to frequent errors and delays.

Defining the Flow

Using research insights, I mapped the end-to-end fleet management journey — from vehicle intake to disposition — and identified the key moments of friction. I redesigned the information architecture to surface the most critical data at a glance.

Design and Prototyping

I designed and prototyped the core fleet dashboard and vehicle detail views in Figma, iterating based on feedback from usability testing sessions with dealership staff. The final prototype focused on reducing clicks, surfacing status clearly, and supporting bulk actions.
